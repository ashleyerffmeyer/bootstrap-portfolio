About Me

I’m a creative, detail-oriented Software Developer & Engineer looking for new opportunities to make everyday interesting products that reach wide audiences in innovative ways. I love working in an industry where art and technology converge on to make unique experiences for end users and customers. I enjoy fast paced roles where developers and engineers see the effects of their work immediately and make instant changes based on feedback and innovation.

My background is in Mechanical Engineering and Engineering Management. I have 10 years of program management, Six Sigma, and manufacturing experience. I’m passionate about continuous improvement and persistent learning.

In my free time, I love to eat and run my way through my adopted hometown of Kansas City. I look forward to Restaurant Week and Rock the Parkway half marathon weekend every year! When I am not out exploring KC, I’m hanging out with my black and tan dachshund named Chunk.
